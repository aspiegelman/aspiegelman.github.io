Welcome to my homepage!

All you need to know about living a gluten free lifestyle as a college student in Madison, Wisconsin!

My background:

  • I am from Long Island, New York. I live in a town called Roslyn which is about 40 minutes outside of New York City.
  • In 2016, I was diagnosed with Celiac Disease. Since then, I have been on a journey to create gluten free recipes and find the best gluten free restaurants around. I am currently a sophomore at UW Madison. Navigating around Madison to find gluten free food was a challenge at first, but after much exploring, I have adjusted to this city and I am here to share everything I have learned with you!
